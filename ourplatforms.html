Immigration

We will support immigrants and refugees by providing them with the same benefits as citizens.

Banning people from certain countries from coming to America does not make sense because not everyone is a threat. It is a huge assumption to say that everyone wanting to come here is a terrorist or possible gang member. Immigrants are “makers”, not “takers”. Immigrants (including undocumented ones) pay taxes In 2010, undocumented immigrants paid about $11.2 billion dollars in state and local taxes. Immigrants are beneficial to the US economy. Instead of working to deport undocumented immigrants, we should work to make them citizens.
